Functional Constipation:
1. Straining in >25% of defecations
2. Lumpy/hard stools (Bristol Stool Scale types 1-2) in >25% of defecations
3. Feeling of incomplete evacuation in >25% of defecations
4. Sensation of anorectal blockage in >25% of defecations
5. Manual maneuvers required in >25% of defecations
6. <3 spontaneous bowel movements (SBM) per week
7. Rare occurrence of loose stools without laxatives
8. Symptoms not meeting Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS) criteria
- Must be present for the last 3 months, with a symptom onset at least 6 months before diagnosis

Functional Diarrhea:
1. >25% of stools are loose or watery
2. No predominant abdominal pain/bloating
3. Excludes diarrhea-predominant IBS (IBS-D)
- Criteria met for the last 3 months, with symptom onset at least 6 months before diagnosis

Functional Abdominal Bloating/Distension:
1. Recurrent bloating/distension (>1 day/week on average) is the predominant symptom
2. Excludes IBS, functional constipation, diarrhea, or postprandial distress syndrome
3. Possible mild pain associated with bloating and minor bowel movement abnormalities
- Must be present for the last 3 months with symptom onset at least 6 months prior to diagnosis

Unspecified Functional Bowel Disorder:
1. Bowel symptoms not explained by an organic cause
2. Does not meet criteria for IBS, functional constipation, diarrhea, or abdominal bloating/distension
- Criterion met for the last 3 months with symptom onset at least 6 months before diagnosis

Opioid-Induced Constipation:
1. New or worsening constipation symptoms with the initiation/change/increase of opioid therapy; includes two or more of the following:
   a. Straining in >25% of defecations
   b. Lumpy/hard stools (Bristol Stool Scale types 1-2) in >25% of defecations
   c. Sensation of incomplete evacuation in >25% of defecations
   d. Sensation of anorectal blockage in >25% of defecations
   e. Manual maneuvers required in >25% of defecations
   f. Fewer than three SBM per week
2. Rare occurrence of loose stools without laxatives

Source: ROME IV Diagnostic Criteria for DGBIs 16 January 2016.
