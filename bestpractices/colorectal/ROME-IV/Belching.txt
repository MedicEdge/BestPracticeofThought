**Belching Disorders**

Diagnostic criteria:
- Bothersome belching from the esophagus or stomach occurring more than 3 days a week.

**Excessive Supragastric Belching (From Esophagus)**
Supportive criteria:
1. Frequent, repetitive belching observed.
2. Requires objective intraluminal impedance measurement to distinguish from gastric belching.

**Excessive Gastric Belching (From Stomach)**
Supportive criteria:
1. Gastric belching has no established clinical correlate apart from excessive belching itself.

Criteria for both types must be fulfilled for the last 3 months with symptom onset at least 6 months prior to diagnosis.

Source: ROME IV Diagnostic Criteria for DGBIs 16 January 2016.
